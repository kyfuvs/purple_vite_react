十二宮位 (12 Palaces)
命宮 (Ming Gong): Self Palace
兄弟宮 (Xiong Di Gong): Siblings Palace
夫妻宮 (Fu Qi Gong): Spouse Palace
子女宮 (Zi Nu Gong): Children Palace
財帛宮 (Cai Bo Gong): Wealth Palace
疾厄宮 (Ji E Gong): Health Palace
遷移宮 (Qian Yi Gong): Travel Palace
交友宮 (Jiao You Gong): Friends Palace / Subordinate Palace
官祿宮 (Guan Lu Gong): Career Palace
田宅宮 (Tian Zhai Gong): Property Palace
福德宮 (Fu De Gong): Mental Palace / Fortune Palace
父母宮 (Fu Mu Gong): Parents Palace
